After the annual-album run, the pace changed and so did the sound.
Singles led. Experiments landed — melodic, minimal, sometimes barely
rap at all — and the *999* EP (2020) and *Carpe Diem* (2020) reframed
what an Olamide project could be.

This chapter follows the risk-taking: what he dropped, what he picked
up, and why the reinvention worked.
