The self-titled chapter. When an artist puts their own name on the cover,
they're telling you the story has reached the part where it's about who
they are. *Olamidé* arrived fourteen years after a debut that needed a
young Wizkid on the hook to cross over — and this time "Kai!" reunites
them as peers.

Seventeen tracks stretch from Bariga doctrine to international guests:
Dr. Dre and Spinall on "Indika," Popcaan on "Rain," Asake and Seyi Vibez
on "99." The archive doesn't end here. It just finally carries his name
on the spine.
