Undefeated energy, named after the boxer who never lost. Fifth solo album
in five consecutive years — a pace critics called ridiculous and fans
treated as expected. *Eyan Mayweather* arrived months after the Phyno
collab *2 Kings*, closing 2015 with twenty-one tracks of street anthems
and softer turns.

"Bobo," "Melo Melo," "Lagos Boys" and "Matters Arising" had already done
the rollout work; the album collected them into a statement of consistency.
Album of the Year at the NEAs followed. Whether every punch landed was
debated — whether he was still in the ring was not.
