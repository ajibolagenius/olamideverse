Texture & Edges

Paste-up

Cards tilt ±0.5–1.5° with hard offset shadows — posters glued to a wall. The grid stays rational underneath; the tilt is the accent.

Grain

Dark sections carry 3–6% static SVG noise. Film grain, not glitter — it should read as print, never as an effect.
