A decade-plus in, still refusing to be predictable. *Unruly* is the label-boss
album in the literal sense: fifteen tracks built around the artists he'd
raised and the peers who defined the new mainstream — Asake, Fireboy DML,
Bnxn, Rema, CKay.

"Trumpet" and "New Religion" led the singles campaign. Between them sits
the thesis of the elder-statesman era: less the loudest voice on every
record, more the reason the record exists. Retirement talk had returned
in 2022; *Unruly* answered the way he always had — with another project.
