The reinvention lands. Melody-forward, unhurried, and one of the
most-streamed chapters of the catalog. *Carpe Diem* was the first fruit of
the YBNL × Empire deal — twelve tracks recorded that July and released into
an October defined by #EndSARS as much as by rollout calendars.

"Eru" and "Green Light" led; "Infinity" with Omah Lay became the permanent
single. Guests spanned the new wave — Bella Shmurda, Bad Boy Timz, Peruzzi,
Fireboy DML — with Phyno as the through-line from the street-hop years. By
mid-2021 the album had crossed half a billion streams: seize the day,
measured in plays.
