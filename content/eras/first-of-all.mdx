Leaving Coded Tunes could have been the end of the story. Instead
Olamide founded his own label — YBNL Nation, named for the *Yahoo Boy
No Laptop* mixtape energy — and went independent at the exact moment
the Nigerian industry said you couldn't.

*YBNL* (2012) and *Baddest Guy Ever Liveth* (2013) landed back to back,
and the takeover stopped being a prediction. This is the era of the
label boss in the making: the hits, the features, the first signings.
