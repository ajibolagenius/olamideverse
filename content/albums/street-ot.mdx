The annual-album run begins. Made for the street that made him — and titled
like a dedication. *Street OT* is Olamide's fourth studio album, twenty-two
tracks deep, stacked with the YBNL next wave (Lil Kesh, Chinko Ekun, Viktoh)
alongside Pasuma, Don Jazzy, Phyno and Reminisce.

"Story For The Gods" and "Goons Mi" led the charge; the Headies answered
with Album of the Year. More than hardware, the record set the tempo for
the Street King years: industrial output, street-first features, and the
proof that street-hop could headline without diluting the accent that got
it there.
